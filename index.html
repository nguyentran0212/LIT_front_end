evaluate your innovation

So, you have built an innovative application for the Internet of Things. You conducted all the tests, and it performed marvellously in the lab. Congratulations! Now, it is time to show it to the world. But wait, how would know how your application would work on other Internet of Things infrastructure? And how would you convince people to believe in your results?

Our Ledger-enabled Internet of Things Testbed might just be the answer you are looking for.

Test anywhere

LIT is built upon an IoT testbed infrastructure that spans Australia. Upon launching, LIT provides access to not just a wide range of hardware, but also a wide range of locations to conduct your tests.

Secured

All of the experiment runs are controlled by cryptographically linked keys. No one but you, who own the key can sign a transaction to conduct an experiment.

Immutable Record

Life cycle record of your experiment lives on our blockchain. Immutable. Forever. While the experiment might not be replicated, immutable record is pretty good.

Ready in 10 minutes

Built on a private ledger that has been tuned for performance, LIT aims to record and deploy your application in less than 10 minutes.
